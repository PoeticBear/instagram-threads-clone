Tweet is a fast, focused social app for sharing text and photos with the people who matter most to you. Inspired by the conversation-first web, Tweet puts your thoughts at the center — no algorithms deciding what you see, just the people you chose to follow.

KEY FEATURES

• Compose posts with text, photos, and locations
• Follow other users and see updates in chronological order
• Real-time notifications for likes, replies, mentions, and follows
• Camera integration with filters and editing tools
• Find people and topics through a lightweight search
• Privacy-first design — your contacts stay on your device

WHY TWEET

The best conversations on the internet happen in public, in the open. Tweet keeps it that way: text-first, image-friendly, and built for the moment. Whether you want to share a quick thought, start a thread, or follow breaking news in real time, Tweet keeps you in the conversation.

BUILT FOR IOS

Tweet is built natively for iPhone with a clean, fast Flutter UI. Sign in with Apple, Google, your phone number, or a username — whichever feels right.
